Shengyu Huang 黄盛宇

I am a 4th-year PhD student in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing Lab of ETH Zürich, jointly supervised by Konrad Schindler and Andreas Wieser. I am currently working as a Student Researcher with Federico Tombari at Google. Previously, I was a Research Scientist Intern with Sanja Fidler at NVIDIA Toronto AI Lab.

I obtained my master degree in Geomatics Engineering from ETH Zürich. My undergraduate study was done in Shanghai, where I spent my first year in Mathematics but changed my direction and completed a bachelor in Surveying and Mapping Engineering at Tongji University.

I co-organize the ETH Geomatics Seminar, please reach out if you are interested in presenting. The previous grogram can be found here.

I will graduate in autumn 2024 and I am looking for industry positions or Post-doc opportunities. Please reach out if you have openings and find me might be a fit.
